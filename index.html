About the Project

NutriAI offers a customized meal planner developed by Solar System Devs that helps users with their eating habits to manage their dietary needs efficiently and effectively. It is designed to provide personalized meal recommendations based on individual health data, preferences, and goals, making nutrition management easier and more accessible for users. NutriAI allows the users to specify their health conditions, dietary restrictions, and nutrition goals to address or to maintain a healthy diet. Overall, this project seeks to transform the way people make meal planning, making it easier, more convenient and effective in improving overall health and well - being.

Core Features

AI generated meal plans

AI-generated meal suggestions can be a powerful feature for enhancing meal planning and food experiences. With AI, meal suggestions can be tailored to individual preferences, dietary restrictions, and nutritional goals. By analyzing past meals, ingredient availability, and personal taste preferences, AI can suggest diverse and balanced meal options that save time and reduce decision fatigue.

BMi Calculator

This tool is useful for promoting awareness of general wellness, offering an easy-to-understand metric that can encourage positive lifestyle changes. By including additional information such as age, gender, and activity level, a BMI calculator can become even more personalized, serving as a helpful starting point for users looking to improve or maintain their health.

Social forum

A forum feature is an excellent addition for building a community and facilitating user engagement. It creates a space for users to share knowledge, ask questions, and connect with others who have similar interests or experiences.
